A special session as a part of the regular Semiclassical Online Network Exchange (SCONE) seminar series will be devoted to a panel discussion on the BH information loss puzzle/paradox on Monday 1 December 2025 at 2 pm UK time online.

The panel consists of:
Prof Bernard Kay (York, UK)
Prof Suvrat Raju (ICTS-TIFR, India)
Prof Carlo Rovelli (Marseille, France)

The panel will be moderated by Dr Erik Curiel (LMU, Germany).

Connection details will be shared via the mailing list near to the date.
