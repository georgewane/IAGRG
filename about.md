      <h2>Who We Are</h2>
      <p>
        The Indian Association for General Relativity and Gravitation (IAGRG)
        brings together researchers, educators, and students engaged in the
        study of gravity and spacetime. It serves as a common platform for
        scientific exchange, community building, and the advancement of
        gravitational science in India.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="about-row">
    <div class="about-accent"></div>
    <div class="about-content">
      <h2>History & Mission</h2>
      <p>
        In February 1969, Indian relativists met in Ahmedabad to honour
        Professor V. V. Narlikar. During this meeting, Professor P. C. Vaidya
        proposed the formation of a society of Indian relativists, leading to
        the birth of IAGRG, with Professor V. V. Narlikar as Founder President.
      </p>
      <p>
        The mission of IAGRG is to promote interest in gravitation and support
        research, dialogue, and scientific development in related areas.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
